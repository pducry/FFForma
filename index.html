FFForma is a creative side project by Pedro Julien — a parallel design practice with an education focus, dedicated to the creative integration between humans and artificial intelligence. Two fronts working together: delivering innovative projects with maximum personality, and bringing AI into the creative industry for real through hands-on education.

Working with agencies, studios, teams, and creative spaces — as well as individual creatives, freelancers, and students — to implement generative systems, agent flows, and trained skills into their workflows. Unlocking new creative directions, compressing iteration cycles, and teaching how to use AI creatively through hands-on frameworks. Not theory. Practice.

Five steps. How a brief becomes a delivered project — agents, skills, and craft working together.

01 Decode
⬡ Agent Flow

Before a single pixel, a research agent crawls market, competitors, and audience signals — building a structured intelligence brief with positioning gaps and opportunity maps.

A sharp, data-backed brief before the first call. No guessing.

02 Frame
⚡ Skill-Based

Direction, scope, and success criteria locked in one call. Custom skills auto-generate timelines, deliverable specs, and review cadences from the brief — zero admin overhead.

The project framework adapts to scope. Not a template — a trained configuration.

03 Explore
⬡ Agent Flow

Where AI changes everything. Parallel agents generate 30-50+ creative directions — naming systems, visual concepts, layout variations, type pairings — in hours, not weeks. The best ideas are curated, combined, and pushed forward.

Massive creative exploration. Human taste selects. AI volume enables.

04 Sharpen
⚡ Skill-Based

Feedback flows through structured review skills — every comment logged, every revision tracked, every decision documented. No thread-chasing, no lost context.

Trained review loops that learn preferences and accelerate convergence.

05 Ship
⬡ Agent Flow

A delivery agent compiles production-ready assets, generates brand guidelines, builds component libraries, and packages everything into a handoff system the team can actually use.

Not a zip file. A living framework ready for the next move.

Every project gets an adapted framework — agents, skills, and models configured to its specific needs. No two processes are identical.

FFForma Learning Process — teaching creative teams to work with AI. Not a generic course — a hands-on program built from the same frameworks used to deliver creative projects. Learn by doing, with real briefs and real tools — boosting the creative process and recognizing practical opportunities to evolve the way you design.

01 Understand
⬡ Foundation

Starts by mapping where the team is — tools, process, gaps. Then the fundamentals: how agents work, what skills are, and where GenAI actually fits in a creative workflow.

No assumptions. Starting from where you are.

02 Build
⚡ Hands-on

Building the first agent flows and custom skills together — applied to real projects. Learning by configuring, not watching. Every framework tailored to the team's context.

You build it. Guided process.

03 Explore
⬡ Creative

This is where it clicks. Using AI for massive creative exploration — generating dozens of directions, curating with taste, and pushing the best ideas forward. Volume meets craft.

AI gives you range. Your eye does the editing.

04 Integrate
⚡ Workflow

Embedding what's been learned into the daily workflow — review loops, delivery pipelines, team rituals. AI stops being a side experiment and becomes part of how the team works.

Not a workshop that ends. A new way of working that stays.

05 Evolve
⬡ Ongoing

AI moves fast. Teaching teams how to keep up — updating frameworks, testing new models, adapting skills as tools evolve. The goal is autonomy, not dependency.

Learning to fish. With agents.
